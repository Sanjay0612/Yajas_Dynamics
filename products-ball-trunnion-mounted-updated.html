Trunnion Mounted Ball Valve

Trunnion mounted ball valves are designed for large diameter and high-pressure applications. The trunnion support system reduces stress on seals and ensures superior performance in the most demanding industrial environments.

Trunnion Mounted Ball Valve Categories

Choose from our range of trunnion mounted ball valve configurations

2 Pieces WCB Trunnion Mounted Ball Valve

Two-piece carbon steel trunnion mounted valves with split body design for easy maintenance and serviceability. Ideal for general industrial high-pressure applications.

  • Size Range: NPS 2" ~ 14" (DN 50 ~ 350)
  • Pressure: Class 150LB - 2500LB
  • Material: WCB (Carbon Steel)
  • Easy maintenance design
Connection: Flange (RF, FF, RTJ), Butt Welded (BW), Socket Welded (SW)

3 Pieces Trunnion Mounted Ball Valve

Three-piece modular design offering maximum flexibility with removable middle section for complete internal maintenance without removing the valve body.

  • Size Range: NPS 2" ~ 14" (DN 50 ~ 350)
  • Pressure: Class 150LB - 2500LB
  • Modular construction
  • Internal maintenance capability
Connection: Flange (RF, FF, RTJ), Butt Welded (BW), Socket Welded (SW)

Big Size Flange Trunnion Mounted Ball Valve
