Gate Valves

Gate valves are designed for on/off isolation of fluid flow. They feature a wedge-shaped closure element that moves perpendicular to the direction of flow, providing minimal flow restriction when fully open.

Key Features

  • Minimal flow restriction
  • Bidirectional sealing
  • Long service life
  • Cast steel construction
  • Reliable on/off control
